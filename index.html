Frequently Asked Questions

What is the price range for these independent builder floors?
250 Gaj: ₹2.3 Cr – ₹3 Cr | 350 Gaj: ₹2.8 Cr – ₹3.9 Cr | 500 Gaj: ₹3.35 Cr – ₹4.75 Cr. Prices are all-inclusive with no hidden charges. Call us for exact pricing based on sector and floor preference.
Are these plots government allotted?
Yes! All plots are allotted by the Haryana Government under HUDA (now DGTCP/HRERA). This ensures complete paperwork, government-backed infrastructure, and zero legal hassles. All documentation is verifiable and transparent.
What is the possession timeline?
We have both Ready-to-Move and under-construction options. Under-construction 250/350 Gaj floors take 3–4 months. 500 Gaj floors take 6–7 months. Ready-to-Move units are available for immediate possession.
Which sectors are available and how is the connectivity?
Available in Sectors 21A, 21B, 21C, 46, 28, 19, 31, 16, 17 and more. All sectors are well-connected via Delhi-Mathura Road & Surajkund Road with quick access to South Delhi, Gurgaon, and Noida. Metro & Railway Station are nearby.
Is home loan available?
Yes! These properties are eligible for home loans from all major banks including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis Bank, and PNB. We assist with loan documentation and processing at no extra charge.
What is included in "semi-furnished"?
Semi-furnished includes: Modular kitchen with granite countertop, wardrobes in all bedrooms, air conditioner provision (power points + concealed wiring), premium tile flooring, quality bathroom fixtures, and painted walls. Specific inclusions may vary by unit.
Can I visit the site before deciding?
Absolutely! We encourage site visits. Fill the enquiry form or call us to schedule a free site visit. Our team will pick you up if required and show you available units across sectors.
What makes these floors better than high-rise apartments?
Independent floors offer higher privacy, no monthly society charges, your own stilt parking, private terrace, and the ability to customise. They are in a non-industrial residential zone with zero noise/pollution — something high-rises rarely offer. Additionally, they have stronger resale and rental demand in Faridabad HUDA sectors.
